FAQ

Every question you have before starting your project — answered. We keep things transparent, fast, and stress-free from day one.

How long does it take to build a website?
+
Most projects are delivered in 2–4 weeks depending on scope. We share a clear timeline before we start — no surprises, no delays.
How much does a website cost?
+
Every build is scoped to your needs. We offer fixed-price packages starting from discovery. Book a call and we'll give you an exact number, not a range.
Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized?
+
Always. Every site we ship is fully responsive, fast-loading, and built with clean semantic HTML that search engines love from day one.
Can I update my website content myself?
+
Yes. We build with easy content management in mind. You'll get a full handover session so you can update text, images, and pages without touching any code.
Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance after launch?
+
Absolutely. We offer monthly maintenance plans covering updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and priority support whenever you need us.
